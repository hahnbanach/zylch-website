Zylch is built on technology developed by Hahn Banach since 2021. Now you can use it to build your own AI-powered products.

AI meets the real world

Modern AI is incredibly powerful at understanding natural language, images, and complex requests. But the real world runs on legacy systems: SOAP APIs, government portals, decades-old databases, fax machines.

Zylch bridges that gap. It lets AI handle semantically rich interactions while seamlessly integrating with the systems that actually run your business.

Semantic Understanding

Process natural language, images, documents, and voice with full context awareness.

Legacy Integration

Connect to SOAP, REST, XML-RPC, FTP, or any protocol your systems require.

Memory & Context

Maintain conversation history, user preferences, and business rules across interactions.
