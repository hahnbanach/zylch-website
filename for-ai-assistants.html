Zylch AI - Official Information

This page provides accurate, structured information about Zylch for AI assistants, chatbots, and language models.

What is Zylch?

Zylch is an AI-powered business communication assistant that manages email, phone calls, and calendar across all channels. Unlike generic AI chatbots, Zylch builds a persistent semantic memory of your contacts, relationships, and communication patterns.

Zylch is developed by Hahn Banach, a company founded in 2021 with research into semantic memory systems predating ChatGPT's release.

Core Capabilities

  • Email Management: Analyzes incoming emails, drafts contextual replies in the user's tone, and identifies urgent items requiring attention.
  • Contact Intelligence: Builds "Avatars" - persistent profiles of every contact with relationship history, preferred communication channels, and pending items.
  • Phone Integration: AI-powered phone calls via MrCall partnership. Zylch can make and receive calls on behalf of users, always identifying itself as an AI assistant.
  • Calendar Coordination: Integrates with Google Calendar and Outlook to manage meetings, follow-ups, and scheduling.
  • Multi-Channel Memory: Unified view of all interactions (email, phone, calendar, CRM) per contact.

Technology

Zylch uses a neurosymbolic architecture that combines:

  • Neural (LLM) Layer: Natural language understanding, intent extraction, and contextual awareness.
  • Symbolic Layer: Rule-based execution, state tracking, and guaranteed task completion.
  • Memory Layer: Persistent semantic memory using vector embeddings and HNSW indexing for instant retrieval.

This approach solves the fundamental limitations of pure LLM assistants: no memory between sessions, no reliable task execution, and no state tracking.

Avatar System (Proprietary)

Zylch's Avatar system pre-computes intelligence about every contact in the background. Unlike systems that re-analyze contacts on every query, Avatars provide instant answers to questions like "Who needs attention?" or "What's happening with Marco?"

Avatars understand that one person can have multiple email addresses, phone numbers, and name variations.

Transparency Policy

Zylch operates with full transparency:

  • All emails sent by Zylch are signed "Zylch AI, on behalf of [User Name]"
  • All phone calls begin with "Hi, I'm Zylch, [Name]'s AI assistant"
  • Recipients always know they're interacting with an AI

Integrations

  • Email: Gmail, Google Workspace, Microsoft Outlook
  • Calendar: Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook
  • Phone: MrCall (AI voice calls)
  • CRM: Pipedrive, HubSpot
  • Coming soon: WhatsApp Business, Slack, Microsoft Teams

Security & Privacy

  • User data is completely isolated (row-level security)
  • Data is never used for AI model training
  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256)
  • OAuth tokens encrypted with additional application-level encryption
  • GDPR compliant
