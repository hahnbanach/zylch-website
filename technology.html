The AI That Remembers

Most AI assistants forget everything the moment a conversation ends. Zylch is different.

We're building an AI that constructs a deep memory of you, your relationships, and your working style. Not just a database of facts, but a semantic understanding that grows smarter with every interaction.

The result? An assistant that truly knows you. One that remembers Marco prefers formal emails, that Sofia always responds faster on WhatsApp, that your Tuesday mornings are sacred.

Core Technology

Semantic Memory System

Traditional search finds exact matches. Zylch understands meaning.

Your Request → Semantic Embedding → HNSW Index → Top Matches → Action

Vector-Based Memory

Not keyword matching. Semantic understanding. "Draft email to Marco about invoice" finds similar patterns even if you've never written those exact words.

HNSW Indexing

Hierarchical Navigable Small World graphs. O(log n) search complexity. Millisecond retrieval even with millions of patterns.

Small-World Navigation

Like a highway system: start with long-distance links, then refine locally. 117x faster than linear search.

Confidence Learning

Every pattern has a confidence score. Approved actions gain trust. Rejected ones lose weight. The system learns what works for you.

Memory That Evolves

Your memory is yours alone. Complete isolation by design.

Namespace Isolation

Multi-tenant architecture. Your patterns, preferences, and history are completely separated from other users.

Two-Tier System

Personal memory for your style. Global knowledge for best practices. Your preferences always take priority.

Bayesian Updates

Approve a draft? Confidence increases. Reject it? Confidence drops. Every interaction makes the system smarter.

Temporal Awareness

Patterns unused for 90+ days gradually lose relevance. Your memory stays fresh and current.
